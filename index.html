What is Crusader Kings 3?

It is a strategy game, based in a fairly realistic world. The main goal is to create a dynasty lasting forever. The strategic part of the game is vastly influenced by the feudal system and medieval warfare. Conquering and developing land is crucial in order to maintain or gain power and influence. The player starts out by selecting a holding, which can be a small country, duchy, kingdom or even an entire empire. After that, you simply have to do your best in order for your dynasty to survive.
